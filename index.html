About Me

I’m Sajida Khan, a passionate front-end developer and QA automation expert with over 14 years of experience creating delightful, user-friendly web applications.

My journey began as a curious teen coding fun projects, leading to a career at TrueTech, where I built complex SaaS dashboards, streamlined CI/CD pipelines, and crafted reusable React components.

As a global mentor, I’ve empowered over 10,000 students across 20+ countries through engaging bootcamps and personalized mentorships, helping them launch dream tech careers.

When I’m not coding, I’m exploring AI, designing automation scripts, and sharing tech tips on my blog. My goal is to make technology fun, accessible, and inspiring for all!
